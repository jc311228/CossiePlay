CosPlay

Its a performance art in which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character Cosplayers often interact to create a subculture and a broader use of the term "cosplay" applies to any costumed role-playing in venues apart from the stage.Dressing up is fun, good for the socially awkward and very creative.
The rapid growth in the number of people cosplaying as a hobby since 1990 has made the phenomenon a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan and some other parts of Asia and in the Western world.
